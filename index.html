For this assignment, there were two pieces that initially caught my attention: Wassily Kandinsky, Jocular Sounds, 1929; and theWilhelm Wagenfeld and Carl Jacob Jucker, Table Lamp, 1924. In Kandinsky's painting, the dynamic assymetry yet sense of balance, and the interplay of the objects as unique shapes standing on their own captivated my imagination. Yet, there was something about the lamp that kept drawing me back to it: the relationship of each unique shape which when fused together formed a unique, and balanced piece.
